# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the
TurboTable community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, colour, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviour includes:**
- Being respectful and considerate in language and actions
- Welcoming newcomers and first-time contributors
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behaviour includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualised language or imagery
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to
**harry@patriaco.ai**. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and
handled with discretion.

Community leaders have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits,
and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
